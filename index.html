I am a researcher and strategist exploring how technology can support authentic communication, eudaimonic well-being, and human connection. I hold an M.S. in Interdisciplinary Information Studies from the University of Tokyo (IIS Lab, advised by Prof. Koji Yatani), where I studied interface design that encourages casual chat in remote-work environments.

My path spans HCI research, strategy consulting at McKinsey & Company, customer-success leadership at a fast-growing startup, and 250+ hours of professional coaching — a combination I bring to designing relational systems that help people and organizations thrive.
